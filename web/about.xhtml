Go Farming

Mission

To create a market place where farmers and buyers can meet, build fast and direct connections and make deals.

What are the benefits and uniqueness of this website?

Benefits for Farmers:

• Establishing networks with buyers

• Selling fruits and vegetables for higher prices

• Getting access to agricultural innovations and information

Benefits for Buyers:

• Establishing fast and direct connections with farmers

• Finding needed volume of agricultural produce

• Improving competition and free market mechanisms
